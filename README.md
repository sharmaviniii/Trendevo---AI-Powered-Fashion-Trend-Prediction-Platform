**🚀 TrendÉvo**
AI-Powered Fashion Trend Prediction & Thrift Marketplace <br>
Link : https://trendevo.onrender.com/static/index.html

**📌 Overview**

TrendÉvo is an AI-driven web platform that combines fashion trend prediction with a thrift marketplace to promote smarter and more sustainable fashion choices.
The system analyzes data from social media, fashion blogs, and sales patterns to predict upcoming trends. These insights are then integrated into a thrift marketplace where users can explore and purchase second-hand clothing aligned with current and future fashion trends.
Additionally, the platform includes an AI chatbot assistant that helps users with recommendations, navigation, and styling guidance, making the experience more interactive and personalized.

**✨ Key** **Features**
🔐 Secure Authentication (User & Brand login)
📊 AI-based Trend Prediction (Prophet, LSTM, CNN/CLIP)
👗 Thrift Marketplace for buying & selling pre-owned fashion
🤖 AI Chatbot for recommendations and assistance
📈 Interactive Dashboards for trend insights
🌱 Sustainability-focused fashion ecosystem
